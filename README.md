### howto
long flash = 5
short flash = 1

leds work as following left to right:
- your score: as above
- dealer's score: as above
- turn indicator: on when it's your turn
- win/loss indicator: fast flashing, you win; slow flashing, you drew; stays on: you lost

buttons work as following left to right:
- deal
- hit
- stand
- reset - at the end of the game
